# Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ git_revision_date }}

## General Information


Frikiteam Docs is a static website that provides free technical documentation. We are committed to protecting your privacy and to being transparent about how we handle any information you may provide.

## Data We Collect

### Automatic Data

- **Server logs:** Technical information such as your IP address, browser, operating system and pages visited. This data is used solely to keep the site running and to improve the experience.
- **Technical cookies:** We only use cookies that are essential for the operation of the site (for example, to remember your light/dark theme preference).

### Voluntary Data

- **Comments and contributions:** If you contribute to the GitHub repository, your information is handled according to GitHub's privacy policy.
- **Contacts:** If you contact us through issues or PRs, your information is handled according to GitHub's policy.

## Use of Data

We use the information collected to:

- Maintain and improve the website
- Analyze traffic and content popularity
- Prevent abuse and attacks

## Sharing Data

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties, except where required by law.

## Cookies

Currently, this site does not use tracking or analytics cookies. We only use technical cookies necessary for the basic operation of the site.

If we implement analytics in the future, we will inform you clearly and obtain your consent.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request the deletion of your data
- Object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, get in touch through [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/rasty94/Frikiteam-docs/issues).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on the site or through GitHub.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an [issue on GitHub](https://github.com/rasty94/Frikiteam-docs/issues) or contact the maintainer.
